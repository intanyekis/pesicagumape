A Brief Bio

Corey Todd Taylor was born on December 8, 1973, in Des Moines, Iowa. He spent his formative years in the city before leaving to pursue his passion for music. Taylor had a difficult childhood, with his parents going through a messy divorce when he was just a child. He was raised by his mother and grandmother. Music provided an escape from the pain and turmoil that surrounded him.

Age and Personal Life

Corey Taylor is currently 47 years old. He has been married twice, first to Scarlett Stone in 2004 and then to Stephanie Luby in 2009. Career

Corey Taylor started his music career as a teenager, playing in various local bands before joining Slipknot in 1997. The band’s unique sound and appearance quickly gained attention, and they rose to fame with their self-titled debut album in 1999. Slipknot has since released six more successful albums, including the Grammy-nominated “All Hope is Gone.”

In addition to his work with Slipknot, Taylor also fronts the rock band Stone Sour. He has released six albums with Stone Sour, earning critical acclaim and commercial success.
