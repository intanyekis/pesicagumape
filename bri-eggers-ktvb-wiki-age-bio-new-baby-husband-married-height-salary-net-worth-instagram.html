Bri Eggers Biography – Bri Eggers Wiki

Bri Eggers is a meteorologist with the KTVB First Alert Weather Team in Boise, Idaho, United States. Eggers joined the KTVB weather team in 2018. Prior to joining the KTVB, she spent four years with the 7 News WHDH in Boston, while there Bri earned two Emmy nominations and forecast blizzards, nor’easters, and humid summer days. She also ran the Boston Marathon and started a family. After the birth of her firstborn daughter, she announced that she would be moving back to her hometown Idaho where her family resides. Bri said; “I really want our Charli to grow up with her grandma and grandpa close … and to learn to drive a tractor and learn how to irrigate, and she’ll get those opportunities there.”

She graduated from Kuna High School in Kuna, Idaho, United States. Bri was a Kaveman cheerleader while at Kuna High School. She went to join the San Diego State University in San Diego, California where she graduated with honors. While in college, she took part in the Disney College Program and worked for a semester at EPCOT in Bay Lake, Florida United States.
She as well attended Mississippi State University in Starkville, Mississippi, where she completed the Broadcast Meteorology Program. Bri also studied abroad in Oxford and the Netherlands. Bri is a very active member of the American Meteorological Society as well as the National Weather Association and has earned the NWA seal of approval.

During her free time, Bri enjoys spending time with her family, her husband, Sean, and their baby daughter, Charli JoAnn. In 2016, she ran the 2016 Boston Marathon to raise money for suicide prevention and mental health awareness. According to her Instagram bio, Bri is a fan of cats and Huey Lewis.
