Are Rachel Bilson And Bill Hader An Item?

Celebs Rachel Bilson and Bill Hader have fans wondering if the two are more than just friends. They worked together in the past on the movie The To Do List, but that was nearly seven years ago. Now that both are single, it appears that they’ve realized a romantic connection. At least that’s what the rumor mill is spewing out.

Coffee Connection

As E! News reports, “They were photographed getting drinks at a Starbucks in his hometown of Tulsa, Oklahoma on Saturday.” That’s not much to get worked up over, but members of Hader’s family later joined them. About a month ago, the two dined together at an L.A. restaurant.

Co-Stars Reconnect

Bilson was linked to actor Hayden Christensen for years and they share a daughter. Hayden has three daughters with his ex, Maggie Carey, who happens to be the writer and director of The To Do List. Wonder what she’s thinking about all this.

New Year, New Beginnings

As the New Year approaches, we’ll stay tuned to see if Bilson and Hader get serious. A dinner out and a couple of lattes don’t equal everlasting love, but there’s always more to the story than we see. Perhaps they’ve always had a hunch they’d one day get together and now the timing is finally right. Good luck to the couple…that is if they actually are one!
