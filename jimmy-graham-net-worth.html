Jimmy Graham Net Worth and Salary

Jimmy Graham is an American professional football player with a net worth of $12 million. He played for the New Orleans Saints from 2010 to 2014, the Seattle Seahawks from 2015 to 2017, and joined the Green Bay Packers in 2018. Graham has been a Pro Bowler five times and was a second-team All-Pro in 2011 and a first-team All-Pro in 2013. In 2013, he was the NFL receiving touchdowns leader. During his first 9 NFL seasons, Graham had 71 touchdowns and 7,436 receiving yards.

Graham signed a $30 million dollar contract for three years in 2018 with the Green Bay Packers.
