Charlie Puth Biography, Wiki, Age, Height, Family, Net Worth

Charlie Puth is a singer, songwriter, and media personality of the United States. He is famous for his song “See You Again” of 2015 and many other singles. Charlie rose to fame when one of his songs went viral and gathered millions of views within a short span of time. 

Table of Contents

Ever since a child, Charlie has been passionate about singing and songwriting. He went to the Manhattan School of Music Pre-College to learn music. Subsequently, in 2013 Charlie completed graduation at the  Berklee College of Music and began to make music. His first album came in 2016 “Nine Track Mind “ in association with Atlanta Records. 

Apart from music, Charlie is known for tv appearances in shows such as The Voice, Sugar and Can u Sing?. Besides this, charlie has a huge fan following on different social media platforms. He owns a self titled YouTube channel where he uploads songs and vlogs.

Career 

  • After graduating from the Berklee College of Music Charlie formally entered the music industry and began recording songs. Initially, he began uploading songs on his YouTube channel where Charlie got initial success from his viral song. He won an online music competition named “Can You Sing?”. After that, Charlie was signed by Ellen DeGeneres for her label eleveneleven where he released and sung various songs. 
  • In 2015  Charlie released his first debut single “Marvin Gaye” which got top ranking north and south American countries. In the same, he wrote the song “See You Again” which was featured in 2015s Fast and Furious 7. The song was a tribute to the late Paul Walker. 
  • Charlie in 2016 released his first-ever studio album “Nine Track Mind:” which was a commercial hit and it got top-ranked by several rating charts and agencies. 
  • In 2018 he released his second studio album “Voice Notes”. The album was also a commercial success and got several nominations for him. 

Awards and Achievements

  • Charlie in 2015 received the Teen Choice Awards for his song “See You Again”. For the same song, he won three Grammy Awards nominations and a Golden Globe nomination. 
  • In 2017 he received the Joox Thailand Music Awards in the International Artist of the Year category and won a nomination for MTV Video Music Awards for the song “ We don’t talk anymore”. 
  • His second album “Voice Notes” got him another Grammy nomination in 2019.
