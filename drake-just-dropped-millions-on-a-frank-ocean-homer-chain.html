Drake Just Dropped Millions On A Frank Ocean Homer Chain

Drake is flexing his deep pockets again by copping a Frank Ocean Homer Chain rumored to be worth nearly $2 million. The Champagne Papi was sitting courtside as the Raptors took on the Lakers in the rapper's native Toronto rocking the iced-out piece. Drizzy has been on something of a spending spree lately — which has included racks of cash for a few lucky fans.

More accurately, Drake was strutting around in a Sphere Legs High Jewelry Necklace. The chain features pavé links and .33 inch white diamonds on each of the four orbs. Additional diamonds grace the four branches on the head-turning floating pendant. The item, which is currently sold out, weighs a total of 127.5 carats.

The Certified Lover Boy paired the iced-out chain with a Sphere Legs High Jewelry Bracelet. The bracelet features 18-carats of white gold and a weight of 14.23-carats of diamonds. Both pieces belong to the Homer Label, a luxury venture launched by musician Frank Ocean in 2021.

Seeing Drizzy drop a few million shouldn't be a surprise. More than a decade after pursuing a career in music, he continues to chart well. The rapper has also had success in his non-musical business ventures. With a massive real-estate portfolio and the success of his OVO label, Drake has become one of hip-hops richest artists.

These days it looks like Drake isn’t afraid to spend his wealth either. In addition to dropping millions on luxury cars and a private jet —a Boeing 676 — the rapper also gambles millions betting on sporting events. Drizzy bet over $1 million on the Rams and Oddell Beckahm Jr in the Super Bowl and another $275,000 on UFC fighter Jorge Masvidal, though he did take a loss on the latter.

But it’s difficult to write off the Young Papi as out of touch because he keeps generously handing our stacks of cash to a few lucky fans. While on vacation in Turks And Caicos, Drake teamed up with up-and-coming rapper Jack Harlow to bless a musician with 20 racks of cold hard cash.

He did the same thing just weeks earlier when he handed a random fan $10,000.

For what it’s worth, Drake can afford to be generous. The rapper has built an impressive net worth and is reportedly worth north of $200 million.
