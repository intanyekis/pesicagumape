Armando Montelongo – An Insightful Biography

Armando Montelongo Jr, an American chief executive officer, known for his role in the reality TV show “Flip This House” on the A&E channel, has an estimated net worth of over $50 million.

Armando Montelongo’s Fortune: $50 Million

Armando Montelongo gained widespread recognition through his appearance in “Flip This House,” a reality show focused on renovating and rebuilding houses. His net worth predominantly stems from the success of the show and his subsequent entrepreneurial endeavors.

In 2005, he initiated real estate and business seminars, launching the Armando Montelongo Seminar in 2008, which provides free workshops across the United States. His company, Armando Montelongo Companies, based in San Antonio, Texas, also offers real estate education. Additionally, his movie production company, “Armando Montelongo Production,” was established in 2011, producing the feature film “Line of Duty” in 2013.

In 2011, Armando Montelongo Companies was ranked #19 on the Inc. 500 list of the nation’s fastest-growing companies. He is also an author, publishing the book “Flip and Grow Rich,” which recounts his experiences on “Flip The House” and imparts personal life lessons.

Armando Montelongo, married to Veronica for over two decades, is actively involved in sponsoring sports races, including the Indy 500 car race in 2010 and 2011.
