Meet Felix Rossi of Welcome to Chippendales Biography and Worth

Felix Rossi recently starred in “Welcome to Chippendales,” set for a November 22, 2022 release on Hulu. After receiving professional training in drama schools and achieving success in Argentina, he relocated to Los Angeles in 2021. Rossi was also featured in a music video alongside Dove Cameron.
