Miya Ali | Muhammad Alis Daughter, Who Is Her Mother?

Miya Ali is one of the nine children of Muhammad Ali, the late renowned boxer and most inspiring personality. Miya, a Notary Signing Agent, is also a single mother with one child. However, the identity of her child’s biological father remains a well guarded secret.

Mia has seven half-siblings from her late father’s side, as well as one adoptive brother. Even after his death, the renowned boxer is still talked about, yet many people are unaware of Miya Ali’s mother.

So, in this essay, we’ll strive to learn a lot about her family history and professional accomplishments. Let’s get started!

Miya Ali, how old are you? Extramarital Affair Between Her Father and Her Mother

Miya is fifty years old. She was born in the United States of America in December 1972. When her father had an adulterous romance with her mother, Patricia Harvell, in the early 1970s, he was still married to his second wife, actress Belinda Boyd. However, Ali and Harvell split up not long after Mia was born. They never got married.

Her mother has kept her life out of the spotlight since her divorce from Ali, making it impossible to discuss her current situation. However, we have learned that Miya is quite close to her mother. She’s posted a few flashback images of her mother on social media. Ali captioned a childhood photo of herself and her mother, Pat, “TBT me and my mommy aka Pat” in February 2017.

Miya Ali was first unaware of her father’s enormous popularity.

Growing up as Muhammad Ali’s daughter, Miya had no concept how famous her father was. Miya revealed at the Marchon Film Fest that she had no idea her father had such a distinct personality. Miya got to experience her father’s popularity years later, when Ali served as the titular bearer of the Olympic flag at the opening ceremony of the 2012 Summer Olympics in London.

The Death of Her Father

Muhammad Ali, the legendary boxer and humanitarian, died in June 2016 at the age of 74. He was admitted to the Phoneix hospital for respiratory problems connected to Parkinson’s condition. On June 3rd, the world lost an iconic sports figure who had won three heavyweight belts during his illustrious boxing career.

Miya spoke about her late father, notably his compassion for those in need, during her Marchon FilmFest interview. She stated there,

“He’s generous. He is a people person. He adores kids. He works for the United Nations as a peace ambassador.”

At the same time, Ali said that one of her father’s favorite sayings is,

“What you do on this Earth is the rent you pay to get into heaven.”

Miya, like her father, is active in social work and enjoys assisting homeless people and supporting various humanitarian causes. She even stated on Instagram, “Helping 1 person at a time.”

Miya Ali was raised with seven siblings.

Ali has six half-sisters and one half-brother as a result of her father’s numerous sexual relationships. In 1964, his father married cocktail waitress Sonji Roi. Their love, however, ended two years after their marriage in 1966.

Ali married his second wife, Belinda Boyd, a year after their divorce. Miya’s four older half-siblings were born from the union: rapper Maryum (born 1968), twins Jamillah and Rasheda (born 1970), and Muhammad Ali Jr., born in 1972.

Khaliah, her other half-sister, was born in 1974 as a result of her father’s extramarital relationship with Aaisha Ali. Ali married actress and model Veronica Porché for the third time. Hana and Laila Ali, their two daughters, were born in December 1977. The Ali siblings are all pretty close to one another.

Miya uploaded a flashback photo of the entire family in November 2016, in which the famed boxer can be seen in between his children.

Miya also has an adoptive brother, Asaad Amin, who was born on March 12, 1986. Ali and his fourth wife Lonnie adopted him when he was five months old.

Her academic achievements

Miya attended Teaneck High School before attending Fairleigh Dickinson University-Metropolitan Campus. Ali went on to study at Andrews University in Berrien Springs, Michigan, after graduating. In addition, from 2018 to 2019, she attended a Holistic Health Coach course at the Institute for Integrative Nutrition.

Miya Ali’s Professional Career: She Held Several Management Positions

In 1999, Muhammad Ali’s daughter obtained her first managerial position at IDT Corporation. She worked as a manager of corporate affairs for the corporation for around four years. She then began working at Florentine Gardens’ The Estate. Ali worked as a catering sales manager there for almost two years (2004-06).

Miya was also promoted to account manager at Master Food Brokers. In 2008, she began working as a sales manager at the Park Ridge Marriott in New Jersey. She worked for a year before earning the same position at New York City’s Room Mate Grace Hotel.

Miya has also worked as a corporate group sales manager at the ink48-A Kimpton Hotel, as a manager at World Venture Marketing Inc, and as a senior sales manager at Isla Beach Resort and Ocean’s Edge.

Ali has also worked as an account director at W Hotels – Times Square and as the director of group sales at Hersha Hospitality Management. She is presently a Brand Ambassador for Laila Ali’s Lifestyle. She is also a nutritionist.

Miya Ali is a single mother with one child: Who is the father of the child?

Mikey (aka Michael) is Ali’s son. While Miya has shared several images of her child on social media, she has yet to address Miky’s biological father.

Mikey graduated in May of this year. His mother announced his graduation on Instagram.

Miya is also the proud grandma to Mikey’s son, Cassius, who was born in January 2018. Her Instagram is full of cute photographs of her son and grandchild.

In terms of her romantic life, Miya is not in a relationship and does not currently have a partner.

Net Worth

In 2023, her net worth is $1 million. Her various responsibilities, spanning from manager to director, have helped her amass such a large fortune. Furthermore, her father Ali had a net worth of $50 million at the time of his death.
