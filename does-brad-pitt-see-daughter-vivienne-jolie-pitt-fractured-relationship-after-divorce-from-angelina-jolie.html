How Often Does Brad Pitt See His Daughter Vivienne Jolie Pitt?

What Did Brad Pitt Say About His Relationship With His Daughter Vivienne Jolie-Pitt?

Brad Pitt has been fairly quiet about his relationships with his six children since splitting from Angelina Jolie. While he has talked about being sober in several interviews, he hasn't gone into details about his kids. And he hasn't said anything about his current relationship with Vivienne Jolie-Pitt.

In the past, however, Brad Pitt has talked about being a good parent. He explained to Details Magazine "I've discovered I don't suck at being a dad" in 2014. And when Jolie and Pitt welcomed twins Knox and Vivienne in 2008, Brad Pitt was ready for the adventure of having six children.

As the actor explained to The Telegraph, "It’s chaos at times, but there’s such joy in the house." He also said, “We were four [children] before, and we got into our rhythms and it worked. Then someone new comes in, and it discombobulates the movements for a moment, but then you settle in again and it just all works. Everyone’s pretty well integrated. It’s not the first time new kids have come in.”

In 2014, Pitt was interviewed by Psychologies Magazine and said something very moving about being a dad. And it seems that he must still believe this. The actor explained, “I care about them more than I care about myself. You see past yourself and become more generous and giving, and wanting only the best for your family.
