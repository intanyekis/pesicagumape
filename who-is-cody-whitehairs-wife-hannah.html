ody Michael Whitehair is an American football offensive lineman for the Chicago Bears of the National Football League (NFL).

Cody Whitehair was born on July 11, 1992 in Abilene, Kansas to RaeLyn and Kevin Whitehair.

Cody Whitehair attended Abilene High School, where he was a two-way lineman for the Cowboys football varsity.

In his senior year, Cody Whitehair was named to PrepStar magazine’s All-Midlands Region team after being credited with 140 pancake blocks and 81 total tackles (41 solo, 40 assisted) with 15 sacks on the season.

After redshirting his first year at Kansas State in 2011, Cody Whitehair became a starter at guard in 2012 and 2013.

Cody Whitehair was selected in the second round, 56th overall by the Chicago Bearsin the 2016 NFL Draft.

Who Is Cody Whitehair’s Wife Hannah?

Cody Whitehair is married to his longtime girlfriend, Hannah. Cody Whitehair and Hannah got married in August 2016 in Fulton Valley Farms, Augusta, KS. Hannah is a flight attendant and they met in a flight.

On September 6, 2019, Cody Whitehair and Hannah gave birth to their first child, a baby girl named Blakely.
