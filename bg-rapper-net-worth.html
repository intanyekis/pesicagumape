BG Rapper is an American rapper and actor with a net worth of around $2 million. BG started his music career at very early age and signed his first record deal at age 12. He signed for True Story and Cash money and this contract went on from 1992 to 1995. At that time BG formed a group with Lil Wayne known as The Hot Boyz.

After that, he recorded several other records including "Chopper City," "Ghetto," "Checkmate," "Living Legend," "Life After Cash Money" and many other albums that he released either as an independent album, collaboration album or mixtapes. Along with these music albums, he was involved in some controversies as including Cash money records and other legal troubles. In these legal issues, he got convicted for gun stealing and drugs keeping. For this crime, he was sentenced to a 14-year prison for illegal gun keeping and witness tampering.
