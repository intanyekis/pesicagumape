Valentina Facts

  • Valentina had started her career as a professional drag queen just 10 months before filming RuPaul’s Drag Race.
  • Her drag name is inspired by ‘Valentina hot sauce’.
  • In an interview, she revealed that Virgen de Guadalupe is her drag mother.
  • She rose to fame after participating in the 9th season of the reality show, RuPaul’s Drag Race.
  • Valentina was eliminated in the 9th episode after she lost a lipsync battle to the song Greedy by Ariana Grande. That moment of the show went viral on social media as she was reluctant to take off a mask covering her mouth as she did not know the lyrics to the song.
