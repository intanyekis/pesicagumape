Joshua Jackson and Jodie Turner Smith were married for four years before Jodie filed for divorce. See photos of the exes here.

Joshua Jackson and Jodie Turner-Smith were one of Hollywood’s favorite couples before their shocking split. The ‘Dawson’s Creek’ alum and the ‘Queen & Slim’ actress first sparked romance rumors in October 2018, when they were spotted getting their PDA on at Usher‘s 40th birthday party. Later that November they were spotted holding hands in Los Angeles.. Around August 2019, it was reported that the two had obtained a marriage license in Beverly Hills. They tied the knot in December 2019, and just a few months later, their daughter was born.

In September 2023, the world was shocked to learn that Jodie filed for divorce from Joshua after four years of marriage. She cited the reason behind their separation as “irreconcilable differences.” Jodie requested joint custody of their daughter, Janie.
