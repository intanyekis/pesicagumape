Rod Wave New Album Release Date: Everything You Need To Know About Nostalgia

Rod Wave, the Florida rapper who has been dominating the charts with his soulful and melodic style, is ready to drop his fifth studio album, ‘Nostalgia’. The project is set to be released on September 15, 2021, via Alamo Records/Sony Music. Here is everything you need to know about Rod Wave’s new album release date and what to expect from ‘Nostalgia’.

The Inspiration Behind ‘Nostalgia’

Rod Wave revealed the title and release date of his upcoming album in an Instagram post on August 16, 2021. He wrote, ” [Sept. 15]… Nostalgia, the album.” In a video clip shared on his official TikTok page, he explained what inspired him to name his album ‘Nostalgia’. He said, “It ain’t the destination, it’s the journey for real. That’s why I named this album, ‘Nostalgia.’ Back then, I was thinking about the future or the past instead of living in the moment.” According to UPROXX, Rod Wave also hinted at the theme of his album in a freestyle over Future’s “Wait For U” with Drake and Tems. He rapped, “I’m just a young n***a with a dream / Trying to make it out this ghetto / But this ghetto made a man out of me / And I can’t forget about my past / That’s why I named my album ‘Nostalgia’.”

The Tracklist And Features Of ‘Nostalgia’

The official tracklist of ‘Nostalgia’ was revealed a day before the album’s release on Apple Music. The album consists of 18 tracks with one feature from 21 Savage. The tracklist is as follows:

  • “Nostalgia”
  • “Long Journey”
  • “Call Your Friends”
  • “HG4”
  • “Come See Me”
  • “Crazy”
  • “Love For A Thug”
  • “Checkmate”
  • ” Fight The Feeling “
  • “Turks & Caicos” Feat. 21 Savage
  • “Boyz Don’t Cry”
  • “Pass You By”
  • “Great Gatsby”
  • “Keep It G”
  • “Love Story/Interlude”
  • “Rap Beef”
  • “Back Lit”
  • “2018”

    • Rod Wave has already released five singles from the album: “Call Your Friends”, “Come See Me”, “Checkmate”, “Boyz Don’t Cry”, and “Fight The Feeling”. These songs showcase Rod Wave’s signature blend of rap and R&B, as well as his emotional and introspective lyrics.

    The Tour And Merch Of ‘Nostalgia’

    To support his new album, Rod Wave will hit the road on the Nostalgia Tour alongside featured guests Ari Lennox, Toosii, G Herbo, and EELmatic in October 2021. The tour will kick off on October 19 in Lincoln, Nebraska, and wrap up on December 4 in San Diego, California. Fans can purchase tickets from Rod Wave’s official website. Rod Wave has also launched a merch line for ‘Nostalgia’, which includes hoodies, t-shirts, hats, and accessories. The merch is available on his online store.

    The Success And Expectations Of ‘Nostalgia’

    Rod Wave has been one of the most successful artists in the rap industry in recent years. He has earned four consecutive top-five albums on the Billboard 200 chart, including two No. 1 albums: SoulFly in 2021 and Beautiful Mind in 2022. He has also scored several hit singles, such as “Street Runner”, “Tombstone”, “Richer” featuring Polo G, and “Heart On Ice”. He has received praise from critics and fans alike for his authentic and relatable storytelling, as well as his versatile and captivating vocals.

    With ‘Nostalgia’, Rod Wave is expected to continue his winning streak and deliver another masterpiece that will resonate with his loyal fanbase and attract new listeners. The album is anticipated to be one of the best rap albums of 2021 and showcase Rod Wave’s growth and maturity as an artist and a person.

    Rod Wave new album release date is September 15, 2021. Don’t miss out on ‘Nostalgia’, the latest project from one of the hottest rappers in the game right now!
