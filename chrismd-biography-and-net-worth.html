ChrisMD Biography And Net Worth

Christopher Michael Dixon, better known online as ChrisMD, is a British YouTuber best known for his football challenges and FIFA pack opening videos.

Christopher Michael Dixon was born on June 10, 1996, in Jersey, in the United Kingdom.

ChrisMD graduated from Victoria College but he did not go to University in the bid to pursue his YouTube career full-time.

He joined the YouTube universe on July 6, 2010, and published his first video, his one and only Call of Duty video, in February of 2011.

The channel initially started off under the username Sirhcchris2010. But along the line, the name has changed and is currently, ChrisMD.

With his unique content over the years, the channel has witnessed some amazing growth in the case of subscription. It currently has a whopping 5.18 million subscribers.

Though Chris Dixon was born in Jersey, he has always lived in London, England.  He has a sister named Kelly and a cousin named Ollie.

ChrisMD Net Worth

The young lad has made some significant wealth from his career. He has a net worth of $3 million.
